SUMMER 2021

Summer 2021 was one of the best summers ever. During the pandemic, a lot of us needed a break from quarantine and all the corona talk. When things slowly opened this summer we finally got a chance to enjoy life in a more normal way after a long time. Here are some pictures and stories that presents out summer this year.

Tukish food served. This is Beyti, a turkish dish of ground beef grilled on a skewer and served wrapped in lavash and topped with tomato sauce and yogurt. In the picture we also see Iskender kebap, consisting og döner.
The beauty of Norway is something else! The water from melted glaciers makes the color of the water pop, giving it a beautiful torqouis touch
Fresh flowers given as gift this summer. Some of the most graceful and gorgeous flowers that we ever laid our eyes on
View of the gloriuous village of Geiranger from the top of Dalsnibba. This is Europe’s highest fjord view from the road
Summer drinks from Espresso house to enjoy the vibes that Aker Brygge has to offer. Lemonise is a fave of many and is popular summer drink.
Kvikklunj! This chocolate bar has been titled as Norways hiking chocolate and is used to provide extra energy in packed lunches during hiking. With the majestic norwegian mountaints behind this picture presents Norway at its best
